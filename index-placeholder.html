May 28, 2016
3PM - 9PM
UW Hub Lawn
4001 E Stevens Way NE
Seattle, WA
Free Parking - Free Admission

ISAUW's signature event, Keraton 2016, is back! Our annual event is the second largest Indonesian festival in the nation and we're inviting YOU to join us.

Experience the authentic Indonesian culture through a number of traditional Indonesian dances, music performances, games, and enjoy a plethora of delicious Indonesian cuisines.
